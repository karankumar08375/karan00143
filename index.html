HISTORY OF FIRE SERVICES IN INDIA

1803
(Bombay)
The Bombay fire brigade
was officially formed and
formally placed under the
police as a part-time
function.

1822
(Calcutta)
The fire service in Calcutta
was organized under the
Calcutta Police.

1893
(Srinagar)
The Fire & Emergency
Services, J&K was
established in the year
1893 as Srinagar Fire
Brigade

1896
(Delhi)
Delhi is believed to have
had a fire brigade in 1867,
but the organized form of
fire station is claimed to
have been started in 1896
and was under the Municipal
Corporation

1908
(Madras)
The Madras city fire
brigade was established by
the Municipal Corporation
of Madras.

1942
(Bangalore)
Fire & Emergency Services
in Karnataka was first
established in the year
1942 in Bangalore South
& North under the
administrative control of
the Police department
